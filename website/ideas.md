### Approach A: Dharma Xerox — Buddhist Punk Zine
**Design Movement:** Post-punk samizdat meets Zen monastery notice board  
**Core Principles:**
- Raw, photocopied texture with deliberate imperfection
- Sacred geometry as structural scaffolding (not decoration)
- Text as primary visual element — typography IS the design
- Annotations as marginalia, handwritten-feeling gloss

**Color Philosophy:** Off-white newsprint (#F5F0E8) ground with deep sumi ink black (#1A1410). One accent: a bruised saffron/turmeric (#C8860A) for annotations and sacred marks. Occasional red-stamp vermillion (#C0392B) for emphasis. The palette is a Tibetan thangka that's been left in a squat.

**Layout Paradigm:** Single long scroll, left-heavy. Chapter headers break the column with full-bleed black bars. Text runs in a slightly narrowed column (like a zine folded in half). Marginalia annotations float to the right rail. Sticky footer is a thin black bar with chapter navigation.

**Signature Elements:**
- Distressed halftone texture overlay on sections
- Buddhist dingmarks (⁂ ❦ ✠ ❋) rendered large as section breaks
- Annotation links styled as rubber-stamp gloss marks in the margin

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover on annotated terms reveals a small tooltip/aside with the link. No flashy transitions — just a quiet reveal, like turning a page.

**Animation:** Minimal. Section headers fade in on scroll. Annotation tooltips appear with a 150ms opacity fade. Nothing bounces.

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Playfair Display` — editorial, slightly archaic, holds the sacred weight
- Body: `IBM Plex Mono` — typewriter, zine-printed, honest
- Annotations: `Caveat` — handwritten feel for the gloss layer

### Approach B: Occupied Temple — Brutalist Dharma
**Design Movement:** Brutalist web + Tibetan Buddhist iconography  
**Core Principles:**
- Hard geometric blocks, no softening
- Black and gold, no compromise
- Text density as spiritual practice
- The annotation is a koan, not a footnote

**Color Philosophy:** Pure black (#000000) background. Gold (#D4A017) for headings and structural lines. White (#FFFFFF) body text. The colors of a monastery at night.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width columns, hard grid. Section headers are full-bleed black-on-gold bars. Body text is wide-set, generous leading.

**Signature Elements:**
- Hard horizontal rules between sections
- Gold drop caps on chapter openings
- Annotation links as inline gold superscripts

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states are immediate — no easing. Click is commitment.

**Animation:** None. Stillness is the practice.

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Bebas Neue` — industrial, loud, uncompromising
- Body: `Source Serif 4` — readable, serious
- Annotations: inline superscript gold numbers

### Approach C: Mimeograph Sutra — Punk Pamphlet
**Design Movement:** 1970s anarchist pamphlet + Zen calligraphy  
**Core Principles:**
- Deliberate visual noise as honesty
- Collage logic: text, marks, white space in tension
- The reader is a practitioner, not a consumer
- Annotations are marginalia, not ads

**Color Philosophy:** Cream paper (#EDE8DC), black ink (#111111), and a single neon accent — electric saffron (#FF9900) — used only for annotation marks. Like a highlighter on a photocopied dharma text.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric. Body text in a left-biased column. Right margin is annotation territory. Chapter numbers are enormous, ghosted behind the text.

**Signature Elements:**
- Giant ghosted chapter numbers (opacity 0.06) behind text
- Annotation marks as inline ◆ glyphs that expand on hover
- Section breaks use the original text's own dingbats (⁂ ❦ ✠) at large scale

**Interaction Philosophy:** Annotations expand inline — a small aside opens below the marked phrase, with the link and a one-line description. No tooltips, no popups.

**Animation:** Annotation asides slide open (200ms ease). Everything else is static.

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Cormorant Garamond` — old, a little worn, carries history
- Body: `Courier Prime` — typewriter, honest, zine-native
- Annotations: `Space Mono` — code-adjacent, clinical contrast

## Chosen Approach: **A — Dharma Xerox**

Playfair Display / IBM Plex Mono / Caveat. Off-white newsprint ground, sumi ink, turmeric annotations. Marginalia in the right rail. Sticky footer chapter nav. Distressed texture. Buddhist dingmarks as section breaks. This is the one.
